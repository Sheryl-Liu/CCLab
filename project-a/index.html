What we know so far about Resonant Ripple?
In the year 4202, Sheryl Liu, a great scientist, made a remarkable discovery under the colorful sky.

The Resonant Ripple defied traditional classifications of life, as it appeared to be a puddle-formed entity that lived wherever human beings gathered. It constantly fluctuates, mimicks the feelings of people who interact with it or around it. Each ripple and wave emanating from its edge reflects the emotional state of the interactor. For example, when someone feels angry, the Resonant Ripple will feel it too and change its color into red and "dance" with lines around it; when sadness prevails, it morphs into soothing, gentle hues and shaking with you if you "touch" it...

This responsiveness to human emotion has led many to believe that the Resonant Ripple possesses an innate understanding of feelings, almost as if it were an empathic being.

Guidelines for human interaction with this creature
When human-beings interact with it, tell it your mood by clicking on the cute buttons on the screen; then, you will see the immediate changes in our little Resonant Ripple that resonant with your feelings rapidly. If you ever "touch" it, by pressing the mouse in reality, you will see the further expression of this kind and caring creature.

And suddenly, a Ripple appeared, glowing softly in the fading light. When he found the creature changing its format alongside his mood, he poured out his heart to the creature. As he did, he felt an overwhelming sense of relief wash over him as if the Ripple had absorbed his pain.
